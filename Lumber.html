About Our Lumber

Our lumber is sourced from high quality forests where we have a large range of multiple different timber species ranging from soft white woods like Koto, and Wawa all the way to our prestigious scarlet species such as mahogany, Edinam and sapele. we handle our timber with the utmost care and strive to ensure that the quality of lumber delivered matches the standard of our clientele.

Our Lumber Products

Our premium hardwood lumber is carefully sourced and processed to ensure durability, strength, and a stunning natural finish. Ideal for furniture, flooring, and construction projects, it provides the perfect blend of beauty and resilience.

Crafted from responsibly harvested timber, our lumber is both environmentally friendly and long-lasting. Whether you're building furniture or structural components, this high-quality wood guarantees excellent performance.

We provide precision-cut lumber that meets industry standards for quality and consistency. With smooth surfaces and uniform dimensions, our lumber is perfect for woodworking, cabinetry, and custom construction needs.

Enhance your woodworking projects with our exotic hardwood lumber. Featuring rich colors and unique grain patterns, this wood is perfect for creating statement pieces in interior design, furniture, and decorative applications.

Designed for strength and reliability, our structural-grade lumber is perfect for framing, beams, and heavy-duty applications. Rigorously inspected for quality, it ensures the safety and stability of your building projects.

Our kiln-dried lumber undergoes a meticulous drying process to reduce moisture content, minimizing warping and shrinkage. This makes it an excellent choice for precision woodworking, cabinetry, and flooring applications.
