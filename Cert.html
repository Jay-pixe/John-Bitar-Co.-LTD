Our Certifications

John Bitar & Company Limited is certified by SCS Global Services for responsible forest management and chain of custody under the standards of the Forest Stewardship Council® (FSC®). Our certification demonstrates our commitment to promoting environmentally sound, socially beneficial, and economically viable management of the world’s forests.

FSC® Controlled Wood – Forest Management

This certification confirms that John Bitar & Co. Ltd meets the requirements for FSC® Controlled Wood in forest management enterprises. For the complete list of covered species and products, please refer to the official FSC database at info.fsc.org.

  • Certificate Code: SCS-CW/FM-008331
  • Trademark License Code: FSC-C084672
  • Standard: FSC-STD-30-010 V2-0
  • Valid from: 18 May 2021
  • Expiry date: 17 May 2026
  • Certified Site: P.O. Box 230, Sefwi Wiawso, Western Region, Ghana
  • This certification confirms our compliance with FSC® Controlled Wood in forest management enterprises.

FSC® Chain of Custody Certification

This Chain of Custody certificate covers the production of solid wood, veneer, engineered wood products, wood for construction, and household articles using the transfer system. It also includes the sale of FSC Controlled Wood.

  • Certificate Code: SCS-COC-008332
  • CW Code: SCS-CW-008332
  • Trademark License Code: FSC-C051183
  • Standard: FSC-STD-40-004
  • Valid from: 10 May 2021
  • Expiry date: 9 May 2026
  • Certified Site: Nana Issah Street, Sefwi Wiawso, Western Region, Ghana
  • Only products sold with an FSC claim are considered FSC-certified.
