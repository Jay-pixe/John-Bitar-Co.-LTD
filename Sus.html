Sustainable Timber Sourcing

As a premier producer of high-quality wood products in Ghana with over six decades of experience, John Bitar & Company Limited is committed to sustainable forest management. Our operations are grounded in international standards and regulations set by the Forestry Commission, ensuring the preservation of our forests for future generations and providing sustainable income for our communities. Our unwavering commitment to sustainability is evident through our certification and adherence to the highest environmental and social standards. It ensures that our products meet the most stringent sustainability requirements, providing our customers with the peace of mind that they are purchasing products that are responsibly sourced and environmentally friendly.

Responsibly Managed Forests

Our timber products come from forests that adhere to sustainable forestry practices, promoting biodiversity and minimizing environmental impact.

Sustainable Practices

We work closely with local communities, supporting economic development and ensuring that our timber sourcing positively impacts the people and regions involved.
