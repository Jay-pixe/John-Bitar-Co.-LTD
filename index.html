Our Timber Production Process

We are a vertically integrated timber products company, planting, growing and harvesting trees, to manufacture sustainable timber products.

Plantation

JCM owns and manages its own plantations which are FSC™ certified. We produce seedlings in our nurseries, plant them out on our land areas, and then maintain and manage these planted forests until harvest ready.

Logs

We harvest logs from our own plantations using modern harvesting equipment and systems. These logs we transport to our timber products manufacturing Warehouses

Manufacturing

We are leading producers of lumber,sliceed veneer, rotary veneer, plywood, layoons Product but to maximise the value from our forests we also produce finger-jointed edge glue panels.

Delivery

With efficient access to Atlantic container ports. We therefore ship our products globally and cost competitively, helping our clients to diversify their supply with our high quality products.
